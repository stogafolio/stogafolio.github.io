Apply to The Folio

Complete a Statement of Interest to join our staff.

Any questions regarding the application process? Reach out to Mr. Smith, smithb@tesd.net, and Mrs. Wilson, wilsonc@tesd.net

Statement of Interest Requirements

1. Poetry Critique

Analyze what poetic devices make the poem effective in Marge Piercy's To Be Of Use (a link is available here). Demonstrate your understanding of craft through specific textual evidence.

2. Creative Submission

Submit either: 1) A literary work (story or poem) as a Word document, OR 2) Artwork (illustration, painting, graphic design) as a JPEG file. Multiple submissions are permitted.

3. Teacher Recommendation

An English or Art teacher must complete a brief recommendation form via the provided Google Form link.

How to Apply

All materials must be submitted through our online Forms portal. Please gather your materials before completing the form. A paper copy option is available here.

Statements of Interest are due by the end of the first week in March.
