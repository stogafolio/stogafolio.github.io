Welcome to The Folio, Conestoga High School's literary and art magazine. Every word and image is created by Conestoga students, capturing the depth of each author and writers' inner world.

About The Folio

We are a student-run literary and art publication from Conestoga High School. Although our name has been The Folio only since 2007, we have collected, compiled, designed, and published student-produced art and literature since 1967.

In the issues tab above, take a moment to see how we have evolved!
